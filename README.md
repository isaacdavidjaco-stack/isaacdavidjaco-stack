# Fighter Jet City

A browser game with a city battlefield, magic wand, swords, tanks, allies, and upgrades.

## How to run

1. Open `index.html` in your browser.
2. Or serve the folder with a local web server and open `index.html`.

## Controls

- Arrow keys or WASD: move the jet
- Space: fire bullets
- Q: cast wand magic (after purchase)
- X: sword slash (after purchase)
- B: use bomb
- S: toggle hacks
- Enter: restart after game over

## Shop items

- Hire employee: earns coins automatically
- Buy bomb: clear the sky of enemies and missiles
- Buy wand: cast magic blasts
- Buy boots: move faster
- Buy armor: reduce damage
- Buy sword: slash enemies close by
- Buy car upgrade: faster movement and extra health
- Buy missile guns: stronger shots
- Buy tank support: extra armor and hit points
- Buy state HQ: spawn allies that attack enemies

## Features

- City background with roads and buildings
- Fighter jets, tanks, and monsters
- Wand, sword, boots, armor, missile guns, tank support, and state HQ
- Hack mode toggled with S
- Allies are friendly to the player and hostile to enemies
- Jobs complete by destroying fighter jets
- Coins, shop upgrades, bomb power, and passive income
